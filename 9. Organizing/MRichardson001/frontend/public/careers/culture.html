Spirit of AMLI Award

The prestigious Spirit of AMLI Award is given each quarter to employees who demonstrate passion and commitment to help AMLI reach its goals. Associates, supervisors and senior leaders of the company can nominate someone they believe embodies the AMLI spirit during the previous quarter.
